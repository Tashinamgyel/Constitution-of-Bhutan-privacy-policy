Privacy Policy

Constitution of Bhutan App

Last updated: October 6, 2025
Constitution of Bhutan ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Constitution of Bhutan mobile application (the "App"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

1. Information Collection and Use

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. All content, such as the Constitution text and related features like search, bookmarks, highlights, and read-aloud, is processed locally on your device.

2. Offline Access

The app is designed for offline use. No internet connection is required, and no data is transmitted to any server. Your information stays entirely on your device.

3. Permissions

The app may request limited permissions, such as storage access, only to save your bookmarks and highlights locally. No data leaves your device, and these permissions are solely for enhancing your user experience.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertisements, or tracking tools. We respect your privacy and do not share any information with external parties.

5. Security

Since the app operates offline and does not collect personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being shared or exposed. Your data remains completely private and secure on your device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The latest version will always be available within the app and on our website.
