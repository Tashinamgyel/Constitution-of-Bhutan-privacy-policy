Last updated: October 6, 2025

Constitution of Bhutan (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Constitution of Bhutan mobile application (the “App”).

1. Information Collection and Use

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. All content, such as the Constitution text and related features like search, bookmarks, highlights, and read-aloud, is processed locally on your device.

2. Offline Access

The app is designed for offline use. No internet connection is required, and no data is transmitted to any server.

3. Permissions

The app may request limited permissions, such as storage access, only to save your bookmarks and highlights locally. No data leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, ads, or tracking tools.

5. Security

Since the app operates offline and does not collect personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being shared or exposed.
